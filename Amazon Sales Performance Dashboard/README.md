## 📊 Project Overview

### 🛍️ Understanding Amazon's Sales Performance

The **Amazon Sales Performance Dashboard** is an Excel-based business intelligence solution designed to analyze revenue trends, product performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

Built using **Microsoft Excel**, this dashboard transforms transactional sales data into an interactive, executive-ready report that supports data-driven decision-making.

It enables stakeholders to:

* Monitor total revenue and order trends
* Identify top-performing products and customers
* Evaluate payment method preferences
* Detect operational bottlenecks in order fulfillment
* Understand seasonal and weekday purchasing behavior

---

## 📌 Business Use Case

This dashboard supports e-commerce decision-makers by addressing critical business questions:

* Which products drive the highest revenue?
* What monthly trends influence performance?
* Which payment methods do customers prefer?
* Are cancellations and pending orders affecting profitability?
* What days generate the highest sales activity?

The insights help improve **marketing strategy, operational efficiency, and revenue optimization**.

## 🧹 Data Cleaning

Data preparation included:

1. Date standardization for time-series analysis
2. Removal of duplicates and blank entries
3. Category consolidation for consistency
4. Currency formatting (₦)
5. Creation of derived columns (Month, Day of Week)

These steps ensured data accuracy and analytical reliability.

## 📈 Key Insights

### 💰🧊 Refrigerators Dominate Revenue Contribution

Refrigerators generated **₦78,000**, significantly outperforming Laptops and Smartphones. High-ticket durable goods are the strongest revenue drivers despite potentially lower order volume.

---

### 📦📉 March Shows Strong Revenue, April Declines Sharply

Revenue peaked at approximately **₦140,000 in March**, followed by a sharp drop in April. The decline mirrors order volume reduction, indicating possible seasonal effects, stock limitations, or reduced promotional activity.

---

### 💳💡 Digital Payments Lead Customer Preference

* PayPal (60 transactions)
* Credit Card (54)
* Debit Card (53)

Customers strongly prefer electronic payment channels, showing high trust in digital transactions.

---

### 🚚📊 Order Fulfillment Requires Improvement

* Completed Orders: 35%
* Pending Orders: 34%
* Cancelled Orders: 31%

Although completed orders lead slightly, **65% of orders are either pending or cancelled**, signaling operational inefficiencies.

---

### 📅📈 Strong Weekday Sales, Weak Saturdays

* Monday: ₦42,975
* Tuesday: ₦42,650
* Saturday: ₦19,625

Purchasing behavior is clearly weekday-driven, likely influenced by work routines or campaign timing.

---

### 📦🔌 Electronics Drive Volume

* Electronics: 348 units
* Clothing: 115 units
* Home Appliances: 110 units

Footwear and Books show significantly lower performance, indicating category imbalance.

---

### 👩‍💼🏆 High-Value Customers Drive Revenue

Olivia Wilson contributed **₦36,170**, highlighting the impact of high-spending customers on total revenue performance.

---

## 🎯 Recommendations

### 🚀 Intensify Marketing for High-Ticket Appliances

Expand promotions, offer installment payments, and bundle warranties for Refrigerators and premium appliances.

---

### 📅 Stabilize Monthly Revenue

Launch targeted campaigns before slow periods to prevent sharp drops like April’s decline.

---

### 💳 Strengthen Digital Payment Incentives

Introduce cashback rewards, loyalty points, or exclusive discounts for PayPal and card payments to increase checkout conversion.

---

### 📦 Improve Order Fulfillment Efficiency

Reduce cancellations and pending backlogs through:

* Better inventory management
* Faster delivery timelines
* Real-time order tracking

---

### 📆 Capitalize on Weekday Momentum

Schedule major campaigns on Mondays and Tuesdays. Introduce Saturday-specific flash sales to improve weekend performance.

---

### 📊 Upsell Electronics

Bundle electronics with accessories and extended warranties to increase average order value.

---

### 👑 Develop Loyalty Programs

Provide VIP benefits to high-value customers like Olivia Wilson to encourage repeat purchases.

---

### 📉 Revitalize Underperforming Categories

Implement targeted campaigns for Books and Footwear, including bundle offers and influencer marketing.

---

## ⚠️ Limitations

* No customer demographic segmentation
* No promotional campaign tracking
* Limited to a single-year dataset
* No marketing attribution data
* Fulfillment data lacks operational breakdown

Future iterations can incorporate these enhancements.
